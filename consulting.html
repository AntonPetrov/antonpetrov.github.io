Consulting

In 2022 I started an RNA bioinformatics consulting company, Riboscope Ltd, that provides scientific input, training, and hands-on support with a broad range of projects in Computational RNA Biology.

  • Are you getting the most out of public RNA databases, such as Rfam and RNAcentral?
  • Do you need a bespoke algorithm for RNA 3D structure analysis?
  • Do you need help creating an RNA-related database or a website?
  • Are you interested in custom RNA data visualisations using R2DT?

Building on 15 years of experience in RNA bioinformatics, I am also happy to assist with projects involving RNA target selection and prioritisation, ncRNA genome annotation, predicting and visualising RNA secondary structure, or assessing the quality of RNA 3D structure predictions.
